Trust & Safety

Honesty is the bedrock for successful trading. Trust empowers relationships to produce mutually beneficial results

Sell4Bids is an open marketplace for all buyers, sellers and bidders. One of our fundamental goals is to build a trustworthy community by deploying transparency in our bleeding edge features. We take the interaction among buyers, sellers and bidders very seriously. Our goal is to make Sell4Bids a highly secure and trustworthy online marketplace.

Rules of Conduct:

Be Friendlyt

Maintain healthy relationships to other party you are dealing with in order to have great interaction experience on Sell4Bids.

Set Honest Prices

Set balanced prices in order to make good deal with the other party. As the product picture depicts its actual price so be honest.

Put the Product in the Picture

Provide transparent details and descriptions about your product.

Be Ethical:

No harassment, no aggression, no threats

We forbid the act of aggression verbal or written and any attempt of harass or threaten any one.

No inequity

Everyone who follows our terms and policies can use our site. Discrimination on the basis of caste, creed and color will not be acceptable.

No Falsification

Show yourself the way you are. We accept no replacement. Impression of other people, other entities, or Sell4Bids employees is not permissible, in spite of association. And posting another person’s personal information in any shape is illegal. 

No defraud

We will eradicate any user who mimics another person or entity, sends unwanted messages to latent buyers, or sends deceptive, false, or hateful communication. If you’re on the receiving end of something suspicious do report Sell4Bids team.

BE Hands-On

We ask affiliate of our community to take their individual wellbeing as critically as we do by making good results about how and where to buy, bid and sell the product.

Don’t share confidential information

Don’t give away personal information such as email, phone number, or address. Use built-in app messaging to communicate securely. Do not make line transfers or send money by mail.

Meet in a reasonable site

Consent to meet in well-known, well-crowded public locations, and let a friend or family member know where you’re going.

Examine items

Please carefully look over and test items before you complete a sale.

Report troubles

If you find something on Sell4Bids that infringes our policy, please report it through the app. If you have an experience with a buyer or seller that violates our community standards, please take the fitting action to uphold your personal wellbeing, and then contact us.

Rate each other

We expect part of our community to get high marks, but our in-app rating tool is your chance to give each other honest feedback on the buying and selling and bidding and help improve that experience for others.

Sell4Bids will take action against identified violators of these Rules of Conduct. If you see something or practice something that doesn’t meet to these values, please inform Sell4Bids team.
